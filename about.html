About Us

Asianet Broadband, a division of Asianet Satellite Communications Private Limited is the largest cable network services company in Kerala since its inception in 1993. A market leader in south India, it is ranked among the top 20 ISP’s in India by TRAI and also as one of the fastest growing ISPs in India. Asianet Broadband uses state of the art DOCSIS and GPON technology that has been adopted by world leaders in the Internet Space

Coverage

Subscriber base spread over Home, SME and corporate segments in Kerala. Range of plans with speeds upto 200Mps & lots of data. With the Asianet Cable TV network as a market leader in Cable TV Distribution, ABB too has a widened its reach across the state.

Asianet Broadband has a bandwidth capacity of more than 12000 Mbps, which is considered among the largest in the state of Kerala among the new generation private ISPs. It can provide complete solutions for Broadband needs of corporates along with Web Solutions, Turnkey Network Solutions, Wireless Networking Solutions, VoIP Solutions, networking solutions and System Integration Solutions. It has a ISO 9001:2008 certification for its projects division in the design, development, installation, service and after sales support of wireless, LAN, WAN network solutions and dedicated internet lease line connectivity. With more than 100 service centres in Kerala, it has a 24 X 7 customer care facility to cater to its valuable customers.
